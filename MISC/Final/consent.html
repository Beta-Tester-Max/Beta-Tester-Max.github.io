INFORMATION CONSENT STATEMENT

2025 (For AICS Website Users)

By submitting your personal information through this website, you agree and consent to the collection, use, processing, and storage of your data by MSWDO for the purpose of evaluating your eligibility and providing assistance under the Assistance to Individuals in Crisis Situations (AICS) Program. You also acknowledge that you have read and understood the Privacy Policy of MSWDO, which outlines how your personal information will be handled. If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from submitting your information through this website. (AICS) website .

Specifically, you acknowledge and agree to the following:
Your personal information will be collected to assess your application for assistance. This may include your full name, address, contact information, family details, income, medical records, and other relevant supporting documents necessary for evaluating your situation. The information you provide will be used solely for these purposes and will be treated with strict confidentiality in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173). Access to your information will be restricted to authorized personnel only.

There may be instances where your information will be shared with relevant government agencies and partner institutions to validate your eligibility or facilitate the delivery of assistance. However, such sharing will be done in compliance with the law and within the limits of what is necessary for the intended purposes.

You have the right to access, update, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data, subject to existing laws and regulations. You may also withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at MSWD Alaminos, though doing so may affect our ability to process your application for assistance.

Your personal information will be stored securely and will only be retained for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes stated above or as required by law. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Submission of your personal information is voluntary. However, failure to provide complete or accurate information may result in delays or the inability to process your request for assistance. MSWD Alaminos Laguna

By proceeding with your application, you confirm that you have read, understood, and consented to the terms outlined in this Information Consent Statement.
